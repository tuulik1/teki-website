FAQ

Can I exchange my coins for real money?
No. Teki is a self-motivation tool, and the coins within it hold no real-world value. They're just intended to help you track and incentivize productivity by working towards rewards you've set for yourself.
Is Teki free to use?
Yes! Teki is completely free to use. You may just see some ads from time to time.
Can I sync my data across devices?
Currently, Teki stores data locally on your Android device to ensure maximum privacy and speed. This means there is no way for you to log in nor sync data.
What are 'One-time' tasks?
One-time tasks are tasks that you intend to do just once. These could be larger long-term goals ("Finish thesis"), or one-off to-do's ("Schedule a doctor's appointment"). These tasks are removed from your task list once completed.
What are 'Endless' tasks?
Endless tasks are habits you intend to do repeatedly ("Drink a glass of water"). These tasks remain on your task list even after being completed.
Is there an iOS version?
Not yet. We're currently focused on making the Android experience the best it can be, but stay tuned for future updates!
